ZHCET AMU

This post is written by Jatin agrawal

Zakir Husain College of Engineering and Technology (ZHCET) is affiliated with Aligarh Muslim University (AMU) in Aligarh, Uttar Pradesh, India. Offering undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various engineering disciplines, ZHCET emphasizes academic excellence, research, and industry relevance. The college boasts modern infrastructure, experienced faculty, and a vibrant campus life with cultural and extracurricular activities. Research and innovation are encouraged through well-equipped laboratories and research centers. A dedicated placement cell supports students in securing job opportunities, and ZHCET maintains a strong alumni network for mentorship and networking.
